ABOUT US

We are a group of 6 friends from IIITDM JABALPUR who wanted to make life easier for not only the customers of our area but also the shopkeepers. So, we designed this website which lists out all essential items that we require in our day to day life along with the information of the shops so that they can be ordered easily from home at reasonable prices. Someday we want to spread this system to entire India where shops of a particular area digitalise their sales.
